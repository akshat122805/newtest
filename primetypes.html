Primitive Types - An Organized Study Guide For Arrays

Primitive data types are basic data types that are built into a programming language and are used to represent simple values. They are often familiar to programmers and are the basis for all other types. Primitive data types cannot be broken down into simpler data types and they are created by the platform or interface that the programmer is using.
